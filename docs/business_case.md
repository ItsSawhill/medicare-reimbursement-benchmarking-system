# Business Case

## Scenario

A payer/provider reimbursement analytics team needs to review synthetic commercial contract rates against Medicare benchmarks before a contract renewal cycle. Leadership wants to understand which providers and procedures are above or below benchmark, which services create the largest financial exposure, and what happens if reimbursement terms change.

## Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Question |
| --- | --- |
| CFO / Finance Leader | What is the total financial variance versus Medicare benchmark? |
| Reimbursement Analyst | Which HCPCS codes are above or below benchmark? |
| Network Contracting | Which providers should be prioritized for negotiation? |
| Business Information Consultant | How should the analysis be packaged for dashboards and leadership reporting? |
| Operations Lead | Where are underpayment or overpayment patterns concentrated? |

## Recommended Actions

1. Review providers above 120% of Medicare with high utilization.
2. Investigate services below 80% of Medicare for access, underpayment, or contract accuracy concerns.
3. Use scenario outputs before approving rate changes.
4. Refresh benchmarks when CMS public files or contract assumptions change.
